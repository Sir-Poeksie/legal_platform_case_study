## Purpose

This document provides a consolidated view of the current status of the Legal Services Digital Platform project.

It serves as a high-level project tracking reference summarizing completed activities, current readiness status, remaining launch dependencies, and overall project progress.

The document should be read alongside the Executive Summary, Testing Validation, Deployment Documentation, and Outcomes & Lessons Learned documentation.

## 1. Project Overview

The Legal Services Digital Platform project involved the end-to-end design, development, testing, and deployment preparation of a bespoke website solution for a South African legal practice.

The project was executed through a structured software delivery lifecycle covering:

* Business analysis
* Requirements engineering
* Solution architecture
* Frontend development
* Backend workflow implementation
* Quality assurance
* Deployment planning
* Infrastructure validation

The resulting platform provides a responsive, SEO-ready digital presence designed to support client acquisition, professional credibility, and future digital growth initiatives.

## 2. Current Project State

The platform has successfully progressed through all primary implementation and validation phases.

Development activities have been completed and the solution has been deployed to a staging environment where testing, validation, and deployment-readiness assessments were performed.

Infrastructure investigations relating to DNS, SMTP, and email deliverability have also been completed.

The remaining activities required before public launch are primarily operational and stakeholder-driven rather than technical development tasks.
